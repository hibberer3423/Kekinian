Kisah Inspiratif

Earning Rp 160 million a month, this is the story of young entrepreneur Hamzah Izzulhaq

Lisa Aljamil

Follow

Jun 16, 2019 Â· 3 min read

1. Since high school I've been interested in opening a business

Hamzah Izzulhaq's interest in opening a business has been apparent since he was in high school. His first business was a distributor of textbooks. From becoming a distributor, he earned Rp 950 thousand for six months. Not satisfied with one business, Hamzah tried his luck in another. He opened a business selling credit and making PIN. The business capital is obtained from savings. Unfortunately, his business went bankrupt because of continuing losses. As if there were no words of despair in his life dictionary, Hamzah then started again running a business. This time he sells snacks and bread. His attempt was quite successful this time.


2. Borrow money for open tutoring

The inspiration for opening tutoring comes when he takes part in the business community of students. At that time, Hamzah was offered a tutoring franchise from the people of Bintang Solusi Mandiri. Hamzah Izzulhaq was interested after reading the prospectus and financial statements. Then he persuaded his father to borrow Rp 70 million for capital. Coupled with his savings of Rp. 5 million, Hamzah bought the franchise in 2011. In fact, the price of this franchise is more expensive, around Rp. 175 million. But because he is good at bargaining, he can pay in installments. The drawback is given gradually every semester. The bimbel business has grown. Profits flowed a lot into Hamzah's pockets. Seeing this potential, Hamzah then opened five more lessons. Every semester he earns a turnover of Rp 360 million.


3. Run a sofa bed business

Although he has enjoyed hundreds of millions of profits, Hamzah Izzulhaq is in fact still looking for what other businesses can be managed and produce cuan for him. He arrived at the business of making a sofa bed located in Tangerang. Hamzah was interested in the business. He then bought the three-month-old business in 2011 and runs it himself. He took the name Picanto as the trademark of his sofa bed. The results are satisfying. Every month he gets a turnover of Rp. 160 million. In 2015, the legal entity of its sofa bed business was changed from CV Hamasa to PT Hamasa Indonesia.


4. Although already rich and successful, education continues Already a lot of money and success, what are you doing in college? Not a few people have such thoughts. But that doesn't apply to Hamzah Izzulhaq. He continued his education by studying at Darma Persada University. The decision to resume education cannot be separated from the role of the mother. His mother who wanted Hamzah got more knowledge. And what her mother said was proven true. Hamzah admitted that he learned a lot about business management from college.

5. Learn from Bob Sadino and Walt Disney Hamzah Izzulhaq claimed to learn a lot from the figures of Bob Sadino and Walt Disney. Many books about successful entrepreneurs have been read, including about Bob Sadino and Walt Disney. He understands that to be successful it must meet with failure first. However, the failure was not immediately lamented and then cured. Entrepreneurs who are already successful actually learn from failure and rise again afterwards. That was a fragment of the story of Hamzah Izzulhaq, a young boss who has a turnover of hundreds of millions each month. The latest news is that Hamzah started to enter the property business. He built a 3,500 square meter Tasik Asri Village housing complex in Tasikmalaya, West Java. Once again Hamzah proved that he could not be satisfied just like that. There are still many dreams and ambitions that need to be realized.
