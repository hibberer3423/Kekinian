Young Girl Behind the Success of Farceee Online Shop

Lisa Aljamil

Follow

Jun 16, 2019 · 3 min read

Starting From A Sneakers

In running a business, Farah actually started from accident. At that time, Farah Farce, who was in junior high, wanted sneakers with good quality. And of course he wants the best product and does not exist in Indonesia. Incidentally when he so wanted his first sneakers, he was offered by a friend. But his order through that friend did not come. After he complained to his friend, he was told to contact the seller of sneakers orders directly. Well, it turns out the seller of sneakers from his friend was in Jakarta and he was not the original seller. A sense of surprise and strange immediately descended on him, but with a smile of curiosity arose with the business run by his friend. #Business ideas also appear to see how their friends sell. He thought why didn't he also sell the product here.


Serious Business By Becoming a Dropshipper

After that incident, Farah met the importers of sneakers directly and offered to be a dropshipper of her product. Although he lacked experience, Farah was smart enough to utilize social #media to market her merchandise. By using typical teenager quotes at the time, he uploaded a photo of the imported goods. It did not take long, Farah Farce also managed to sell the shoes to about 15 pairs that same day.Farah did not play in running her business. He was then more active and diligent in learning business, he was often active through social media both Twitter and #facebook. Over time, the development of the business was even more significant. There are so many orders that he has to handle for his customers. At first it only sold shoes, now it develops and sells many products from abroad. Currently his online shop, Farceee Online Shop sells fashion products, such as bags, shoes and accessories.


Dropshipper and Supplier

The rapid development of his business, making Farah Farce not only limited to being a dropshipper. In fact, he also became a supplier at several other online stores. Many resellers and other online stores also take products from online stores fronted by Farah Farce, Farceee Online Shop. It doesn't even stop there, right now Farah has been able to make its own product brand in the form of shoes with the name Flair Shoes. Why he chose to become a dropshipper, because according to Farah Farce running an online business by being a dropshipper is because the process is simple. Moreover, goods with high quality from abroad, of course promise high profits. While becoming a supplier due to the large market demand and the large opportunities to expand its business market.


Tips for Farah Farce's Success

There are some tips for success in running an online business, especially the online shop that Farah Farce runs. According to him, the most effective strategy in capturing consumers is by maintaining good relations with them. From this good relationship, eventually many consumers become customers. In addition, good service with flexibility in negotiating, professionalism, friendliness and a good work ethic, will be able to maintain the success of an online store in order to continue to exist.
